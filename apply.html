Apply for Help

What to Expect

To apply for a free device, please complete the application form on this page. After the application is sent, expect the followings:

  • Your application will be reviewed by our internal staff. We will notify you via email about your eligibility.
  • We may reach out to you for additional information, questions, etc.
  • A typical refurbishing process takes at minimum 2 weeks, sometimes faster or longer, depending on how complex the refurbishing process is for a particular device and/or the availability of the device being requested.
  • Once the device is ready for pick-up, we will notify you via email. The device can be picked up at one of our affiliated locations below:
    • Pretoria Salon & Spa in the Art Museum neighborhood
    • Vientiane Bistro in the Kensington neighborhood
    • Vientiane Cafe in the University City/West Philadelphia neighborhood
    • The Breakfast Den in the Graduate Hospital neighborhood

How to Apply

Please complete the form on this page. Do your best to provide as much details as possible:

  • Tell us a bit about yourself! We'd love to know a bit about you! (If you're applying on behalf of someone, please provide the information about that person)
  • Tell us why you'd need a device. What type of device (laptop, tablet, or something else)? Is it for school? work? and/or something else? Be as detailed as possible as it will help us more focus on your needs.

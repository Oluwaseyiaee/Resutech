Donation Guidelines

Prior to donating the device, please read our terms & conditions statement and consider the followings:

  • Functionality: The device should be in good working condition, at least can power up. Devices with a cracked screen are accepted.
  • Compatibility: Current modern applications require some level of performance, therefore the device should be less than 12 years old starting from the date of purchase. However, there are exceptions, please send the brand and model information for verification.
  • Security: If possible, the device should be securely wiped of all personal data, including files, passwords, and other sensitive information, to protect the privacy of the owner. If not, not to worry, we will securely wipe it as part of the refurbishing process.
  • Cleanliness: The device should be cleaned and sanitized to prevent the spread of germs, bacteria, or viruses.
  • Accessories: The device should come with necessary accessories such as charging cables and power adapters to ensure its usability and convenience for the recipient. If not, that's OK!
  • Communication: Please communicate clearly and honestly about the condition, history, and expectations of the device, including any defects, damages, or limitations that may affect its functionality.
  • Anonymity: Donations are kept anonymous, unless noted otherwise by the donor. A note can be attached to the device with a name and/or an email and optionally, a thoughtful wish for the donee. The note will be forwarded to the donee.

Refurbishing Process

At a high level, the refurbishing process involves the followings:

  • Replace old and/or broken parts with newer parts on a case-by-case basis. The main goal is to enable the device to perform at an acceptable level for necessary modern applications.
  • Securely wipe all personal data on the storage device, including files, passwords, and other sensitive information, to protect the privacy of the previous owner. Note that any laptop and desktop storage devices is securely wiped prior to repurposing it. However, if the storage device is not able to function well enough, it would typically be replaced with a brand new modern one.
  • If the device can not be refurbished, it will be donated to our affiliated certified recycling company Elemental Inc. where it's properly recycled.

Accepted Devices

At this time, we can only accept the following types of devices (no older than 12 years old) because they're the most useful and popular:

  • Laptops and desktops
  • Smart devices such as e-readers, tablets, mobile phones and virtual voice-assistants (Alexa, Google, Siri, etc.)
  • Computer monitors

Important Note About Apple Devices

Before donating any Apple devices, please make sure to remove/unregister it from owner's iCloud account. Otherwise it won’t be accessible by others if they decide to register it to their own iCloud account. Here’s a written or video instruction on how to remove your devices from your iCloud account. Should you need help, please reach out!
